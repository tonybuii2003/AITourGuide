## Overview
CuratAI is your intelligent companion for exploring art and culture. Discover personalized art tours, detailed artwork insights, and exhibitions.

### Description
With CuratAI, users can effortlessly explore personalized art recommendations and guided tours based on their interests and art preferences. Whether you're captivated by classic masterpieces, innovative modern art, or immersive digital experiences, CuratAI transforms your art journey into an interactive, insightful adventure. Say goodbye to generic museum visits, and hello to curated tours powered by advanced AI and enriched with data from renowned collections like The Met’s.

### App Evaluation
- **Category:** Art & Culture / Entertainment
- **Mobile:** The mobile application delivers quick access to personalized tours and interactive recommendations, leveraging native device features for a seamless experience.
- **Story:** Empowers users to explore art collections, receive guided tours, and gain deep insights into artworks and historical contexts—all through an intuitive AI-driven interface.
- **Market:** Art enthusiasts, museum-goers, cultural explorers, and anyone looking to elevate their art appreciation.
- **Habit:** Regular notifications about art events, new exhibitions, or updated tours foster an engaging and habitual experience, while social sharing features promote community engagement.
- **Scope:** Initially focused on delivering curated art tours and insights, with ample potential for expansion to include real-time exhibition updates and additional museum integrations.
